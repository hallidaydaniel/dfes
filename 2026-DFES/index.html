Scenarios at a glance

Five futures for the North

Together with ERM and our stakeholders we model five distinct pathways to 2050. Four reach net zero by different routes. One does not — it shows what business as usual would look like.

Reference

NPg Reference Scenario

Net zero by 2050

Aggressive rollout of EVs and heat pumps, intensive investment in low-carbon technology, early action from government and engaged customers.

Net zero by 2050

Net zero by 2050
